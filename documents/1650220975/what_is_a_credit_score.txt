1. What is a credit score? 


A credit score is a three digit number that is used to represent a person's creditworthiness. This number is based on a number of factors, including how much debt a person has, how often they make payments on their debt, and how long they have had credit. The higher a person's credit score, the more likely they are to be approved for a loan, and the lower their interest rate will be.
