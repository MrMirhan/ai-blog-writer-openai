What is a credit score and why is it important?

There are many factors that impact your credit score. The most important factors are your payment history, your credit utilization, and your credit history.

Your payment history is the most important factor in your credit score. It accounts for 35% of your credit score. Your payment history is determined by whether you have paid your bills on time, whether you have been late on your payments, and how many delinquent payments you have.

Your credit utilization is the second most important factor in your credit score. It accounts for 30% of your credit score. Your credit utilization is determined by how much of your available credit you are using. You should aim to keep your credit utilization below 30%.
