- 2. How to build credit history? 


Building credit history is essential for anyone who wants to establish a good financial footing. A credit history is a record of how you have repaid your debts in the past. Lenders use this information to decide how likely you are to repay a new loan, and to set terms and interest rates accordingly.

If you don’t have a credit history, it can be difficult to get a loan, or you may have to pay a higher interest rate. There are a few things you can do to build your credit history:

• Use a credit card and make regular payments.

• Pay your bills on time.

• Get a loan and make regular payments.

• Keep your credit utilization low.

Building credit history can take time, but following these tips will help you get started.
