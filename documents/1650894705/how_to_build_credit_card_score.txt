A credit score is a number that reflects your creditworthiness and is based on your credit history. The higher your credit score, the more likely you are to be approved for credit and to receive a lower interest rate on a loan.

Your credit score is calculated using a variety of factors, including your payment history, the amount of debt you owe, the length of your credit history, and the types of credit you use.
