How to get your credit card score back on track


1. What is a credit score? 


Your credit score is a three digit number that lenders use to determine how risky it is to lend you money. The higher your score, the less risky it is for a lender to lend you money and the lower your interest rate will be. Your credit score is based on your credit history, which is a record of how you've repaid your debts in the past.
