- 3. Factors that Affect Mortgage Rates 


The mortgage market is a complex and ever-changing beast. There are myriad factors that can affect mortgage interest rates, from the economy to the political environment. In this article, we’ll explore some of the most important factors that influence mortgage rates.

The Economy

The economy is the biggest driver of mortgage rates. When the economy is strong, interest rates are likely to be high, as lenders want to ensure that they are getting a good return on their investment. When the economy is weak, interest rates are likely to be low, as lenders want to encourage people to borrow money and stimulate the economy.

The Political Environment

The political environment can also have a big impact on mortgage rates. For example, if there is a lot of political instability, it can lead to higher interest rates as lenders become more cautious.
